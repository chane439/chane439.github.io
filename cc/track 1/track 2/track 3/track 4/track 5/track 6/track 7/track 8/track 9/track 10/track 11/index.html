nar·cis·sism
/ˈnärsəˌsizəm/
EXCESSIVE OR EROTIC INTEREST IN ONESELF AND ONE'S PHYSICAL LOOKS. EXTREME SELFISHNESS, WITH A GRANDIOSE VIEW OF ONE'S OWN TALENTS AND CRAVING FOR ADMIRATION, AS CHARACTERIZING A PERSONALITY TYPE. SELF-CENTEREDNESS ARISING FROM FAILURE IN BEING ABLE TO DISTINGUISH THE SELF FROM THE EXTERNAL.
